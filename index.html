About Ambrose Canvas

Located in the heart of Maine's lobster coast, Ambrose Canvas is your trusted partner for professional marine canvas and upholstery solutions. Under the skilled hands of Kegan Ambrose, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern marine-grade materials to protect your investment.

From custom boat covers that withstand Maine's harsh coastal weather to precision-fitted bimini tops for exploring Down East waters, we understand that your vessel deserves protection as reliable as the Maine coast itself. Every project reflects our commitment to quality and durability.
